>>> Award Form Guidelines <<<

1. All directions MUST be followed in order for your club to be considered for the awards that are submitted.

2. All award forms MUST be typed for submission.

3. District Policy Code 19, #9 will be enforced as stated. Circle K Clubs will not be eligible to participate in any award competitions at District Convention unless they have submitted at least nine (9) of the required twelve (12) club monthly reports.

*Reports must be submitted to the District Secretary
postmarked by January 31, 2003.*

4. Elected District Officers will be judging all awards, therefore they are to be excluded from the submission of all award forms. In order to keep the judging process impartial, Elected District Officers may not aid in the submission of these award forms.

5. The Official Signatures sheet MUST be included for consideration of all awards.

6. When submitting your awards, include the award forms as cover sheets with each award, so that there is no confusion on which form is being submitted.
