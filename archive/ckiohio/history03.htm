On January 27, 1973 the Ohio District Board met at John Carroll University and they accepted the resignation of Barry Hintz, District Treasurer, and voted to create the combind office of Secretary/Treasurer effective immediately with Chris Macey serving the remainder fo the term. The action was then ratified by the House of Delegates at the 1973 District Convention in Youngstown.
