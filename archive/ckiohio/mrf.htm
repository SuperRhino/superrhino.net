How it Works...
(Read All the Instructions Before Advancing)

1. Click on the Monthly Report Link below (if a box pops up asking for a password, just click cancel)

2. The Monthly Report Form will load, Fill it out as you normally would

3. When you have finished filling out all the information, Click on the File menu, then Click on Save As...

4. Save the file wherever you want, with whatever name you choose, but remember where you saved it!

5. If you need more room for 'Activities Sponsored During Month'
Then Click on the Appropriate Link below and repeat steps 2-4

6. The Final Step: Attatch ALL of the Saved Monthly Report Forms in an email to the appropriate people

The Appropriate People...

Club Monthly Reports should be sent to the following Ohio District figures by the 5th of each month:

District Secretary - Andrea Macy secretary@ckiohio.org
District Kiwanis Family Chair - Chase Waits KFamilyChair@ckiohio.org
District Service Chair - Angela Taylor ServiceChair@ckiohio.org
Your Lieutenant Governor
Your Kiwanis Advisor
Your Faculty Advisor
AND Your Club President

For Email Addresses of the District Officers go to The District Officers Page
