A Message From Our Governor...

As Governor of the MIGHTY Ohio District of Circle K International, it is my pleasure to welcome you to our district's website. I would like to invite you, the members, to search your site and view the many new resources that this site has to offer you.

For our guests, Circle K is a collegiate service organization, sponsored by Kiwanis International, and is run by students; it is for the student that wants to help others. Our district currently has over 580 members from over 24 colleges and universities. We are proud that in the last few years we have averaged over 10,000 hours of service each year and we have aspirations of doing much, much more.

Once again, on behalf of the Ohio District Board and Technology Chair Ryan Pastorelle, I would like to welcome you to our district website and encourage you to stop by often for updates and new information.

Yours in service,
Brandi M. Bowen
2002-03 Ohio District Governor
