MAC HOUSING

Thanks to the generous efforts of Brian Yacucci, Downtown Kiwanis, and Anne Akeson, the Williams Branch YMCA will be used for housing on Friday night of MAC. We have a gymnasium, two racket ball courts, and an aerobics room for sleeping. All who will be in attendance at MAC are welcome to stay at the YMCA.

One stipulation: Please keep in mind, we need to have the gymnasium and racket ball courts cleared by 7am on Saturday morning. This means anyone who is housed at the YMCA will need to be up by 6:30am. For those who have a REAL problem with getting up this early will sleep in the Aerobics room (this includes all workshop leaders, people who REALLY get cranky without enough sleep, etc.).

*** If you indicated on your registration form that you did NOT need housing, but WOULD like to stay with us at the YMCA instead. Please let Alyson know ASAP.

Book Drive

Our Governor and International Trustee are challenging the Lieutenant Governors to a book drive to be finished at MAC. The Lieutenant Governors are asking you to help in their cause. Collect books around campus and the community, from family, friends, and Kiwanians. Bring the books you've collected to MAC. The books that you bring will be added to the collection of books that your Lieutenant Governor brings. The Governor's books and International Trustee's books will be counted as one collection of books. The Governor or the Lieutenant Governor with the least amount of books in their collection will have to bob for apples in macaroni and cheese. These books will be donated to The Literacy Council of Cincinnati
