What is the new "Project Interclub" Award?

This new award was established to help increase Kiwanis Family Involvement with your club and local Kiwanis, Key Club, Builders Club, K-Kids, AKtion Clubs, and other Circle K Clubs. This award is also known as Project: Interclub. The award will be calculated based on the following:

  • Each year the District Kiwanis Family Chair will make a list of 15 objectives to be completed by January 31st
  • There will be three levels of the Award: Gold, Silver, and Bronze
  • You must complete:
      • (7) of the objectives to receive the BRONZE award
      • (11) of the objectives to receive the SILVER award
      • ALL (15) of the objectives to receive the GOLD award
  • Bare in mind that you must have at least 3 members from your home club in order to complete all of these objectives as official Interclubs


So What are the Criteria?

  1. Interclub with a Circle K club outside of your division
  2. Interclub with a Circle K club outside of the Ohio District
  3. Interclub at a Key Club meeting or project
  4. Hold a local K-Family event
  5. Attend a Builders Club or K-Kids meeting or project
  6. Attend a Key Club division meeting
  7. Attend MAC, SpOT, and District Convention
  8. Attend all division rallies in your division
  9. Attend at least 5 meetings of your sponsoring Kiwanis Club
  10. Interclub with another Kiwanis Club (not in your division)
  11. Interclub with a second Key Club (not at a division meeting)
  12. Attend a Kiwanis Division meeting
  13. Attend a project of any club
  14. Interclub with another Circle K (not at division meeting)
  15. Work on a project with your sponsoring Kiwanis Club
