Running of the Cows Point System



12 pts - For each Summer Monthly Report (May, June, July, Aug) sent in on-time

10 pts - Turning in fall addresses, phone numbers, and e-mail addresses of all officers and committee chairs by OCTOBER 10, 2003

10 pts - For each Monthly Report sent to me on-time (by the 5th of each month)

9 pts - Phone call to District Secretary concerning Circle K

8 pts - Personal letter, or e-mail sent to me about your club or club events (excluding monthly reports)

8 pts - Personal letter, or e-mail sent to me about the responsibilities of club secretary

8 pts - Address changes and corrections sent within 14 days of occurrence

7 pts - Attendance at International Convention

6 pts - For each past due monthly report submitted

6 pts - Any contact online (other than e-mail) concerning Circle K

6 pts - Attendance at a District event (SpOT, MAC, Convention)

5 pts - Attendance at a District Board meeting

5 pts - For being present when the District Secretary officially visit your division

5 pts - Turning in new club officer contact information after club elections



BONUS POINTS


20 pts - Every monthly report in on-time

20 pts - Every monthly report in by January 31, 2004

20 pts - Submitting Kiwanis and Faculty advisor contact information

Other Bonus Points might be award throughout the year
