>>> Past District Dues <<<

  • In 1980 District Dues were $40 per club
  • Dues were increased from $3 to $5 a person
    at the 1986 District Convention
  • The increase to $10/member took affect in 1989



>>> Ohio District Membership Trends <<<

    That year the ODCKI had 244 members.
  • The ODCKI is currently at record high membership level
