A Message From Our Governor...

Welcome to the official website of the Mighty Ohio District of Circle K International! If you are new to Circle K, we are a collegiate service organization with over 12,000 members worldwide. We are a sponsored organization of Kiwanis International and are partnered with other Kiwanis sponsored organizations as Key Club in high school and Builders Club in middle school. In the Ohio District of Circle K, we have approximately 700 members from over 24 colleges and universities. This past year we provided over 16,000 hours of service to our campuses and communities!

This site is designed as a resource for the most important part of the Ohio District: the members. If you come back to this site often and use it wisely, you will find it to be an invaluable source for clubs, members, officers, and chairs. I encourage you to come back often! We are here to serve you!

On behalf of the Ohio District Board of Circle K International and District Technology Chair Andrea Macy, I hope you enjoy your visit and return often for new information, pictures, and other great updates!

Yours in the Spirit of the Mighty Ohio District,
Kimberly M. Wilhelm
2003-2004 Ohio District Governor
governor@ckiohio.org
