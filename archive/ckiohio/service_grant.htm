What is the Service Grant?

The Service Grant was developed and approved by the Alumni Association in 1999. It is designed to help Circle K Clubs in the Ohio District implement a service project with little or no start-up funds from the club. The Alumni Association was created for the Circle K member and through our annual dues collection money is set aside each year to fund this grant.

How can My Club get it?

In order to apply for the Service Grant, clubs MUST provide the following materials:

  • Signed Service Grant Application with the president, secretary, and advisor’s signatures.
  • A detailed description of the proposed service project, including:
  • The organization(s) or need the club will be serving
  • Involvement of the sponsoring Kiwanis club, other Circle K clubs, groups on campus, family members, etc.
  • Example(s) of the PR involved with the project.
  • Creativity is encouraged.
  • A detailed budget of the project, outlining in detail what the Service Grant will fund. For example, supplies such as paint, books for children, refreshments.
  • Club must be in good standing with the Ohio District and with Circle K International.

The enclosed application, filled out completely, with an attached project description and budget must be postmarked no later than October 31, 2002, and mailed to:

Katherine Varney
Service Grant Application
5714 Linworth Road
Columbus, OH 43235

All questions can be directed to Katherine at kvarney@wittenberg.edu

The grant(s) will be awarded at MAC 2002.
