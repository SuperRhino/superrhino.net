What is [cki-ohio]?

cki-ohio is an email reflector powered by Yahoo! Groups. Any time a message gets sent to the reflector's email address, the message is forwarded to each member of the reflector; so if you send a message, nearly 30% of Ohio Circle K'er will recieve it. THIS IS A GREAT WAY TO CONNECT YOUR CLUB TO THE REST OF THE ODCKI!

Do I have to be on My Club's Board to sign up?

NO WAY! cki-ohio's purpose is to communicate with all the members of the Ohio District, NOT just president's or committee's chair...EVERYONE. That's EVERYONE from the Governor herself to the ordinary member.

Do I have to send messages if I sign up?

You don't have to send messages...but you definetly can! Some members just sign up to recieve news updates and so they know what's going on in the ODCKI

Will I get a massive amount of Email each day?

No you won't. It's not too odd to go a full week without recieving a message from [cki-ohio]. On the average, there are about 2-4 Email's sent out a week.

Does it only Forward Email's?

No. The Reflector has it's own webpage that anyone can access. The Webpage is almost like an un-official ODCKI Website. Anyone, including non-members can post messages or pictures or an event on the calendar; Members have the ability to create and interact with surveys, and look up any club in the club database. Also if your Circle K Club has it's own website, you can promote cki-ohio but cutting and pasting some simple HTML code.

How do I sign up for [cki-ohio] NOW!?

It's Easy! Simply Click the Link for [cki-ohio] in the dark gray section on the left side of any page on the ckiohio.org website! Then follow the on-screen instructions carefully. All you need is a FREE Yahoo! ID and you can get one when you click the link to sign up for [cki-ohio]!
