Rules That Govern
Daniel J. Romanello Sr. Club Achievement Award


  1. Each Circle K Club shall use the Standard Form for its entry


  2. Each club must be in good standing with Circle K International and the Ohio District


  3. The report should include activities undertaken during the period between the 2002 and 2003 Ohio District Conventions


  4. The following rules apply to this contest:
    • Use specific statements showing definite accomplishments
    • State exact extent of participation of your club - cooperated, aided, initiated, etc
    • Report must be typed
    • For ease in reading, please duplicate the structure of the questions as listed and answer them as briefly and yet completely as possible. Attach answers on an 8 1/2 X 11 sheets of white paper to the back of the corresponding question sheets. Indicate the continuations of an answer from one paper to another
    • Repetitious reporting of activities, fancy bindings, pictures, etc., will not be taken into consideration

  5. List total service hours for each activity reported. A service hour is defined as sixty (60) minutes of service enacted by one member of Circle K International. List time spent and number of member involved in each area:
    • Planning
    • Travel
    • Actual Project Time
    • Follow-Up

  6. If any area or question does not apply to your individual club, please write N/A in the space provided


  7. Up to two (2) (first and second place) trophies will be given in each division
